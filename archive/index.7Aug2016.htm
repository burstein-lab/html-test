David Burstein's Homepage

Hi, I'm currently a joint postdoc in the labs of Jill Banfield and Jennifer Doudna at UC Berkeley.
I've completed my Ph.D. studies under the supervision of Prof. Tal Pupko, in the George S. Wise Faculty of Life Sciences, Tel Aviv University

I was also a proud fellow of the Edmond J. Safra Bioinformatics Program.

E-mail: davidbur@berkeley.edu


Research interests:

In this stage of my academic life, I find these topics of great interest:

- Microbial communities
- Metagenomics
- Function prediction
- Bacterial genomics
- Machine learning
- Phage-host interaction
- Molecular evolution and phylogeny
