Health:

I'm a healthy and presumably happy cat, my fur is shiny and extremely soft (silky, one might say). I only have few mild physical conditions: I'm about 85% blind (which is 15% sighted), my right ear doesn't really function (not for hearing at least), which made me think at the beginning that all the voices came from my left side, and that was kindda confusing...
Oh yeah, I'm also a (latent) carrier of Feline herpes virus type I (aka Feline viral rhinotracheitis), this is probably what made me mostly blind when I was a kitten, but today it basically means I have a running nose most of the time.
Apart for these, I'm (usually) 100% fine.
